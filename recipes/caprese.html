Caprese Salad

The best and healtiest salad you can ever eat!

When you want to eat some fresh and easy dish, this is the one for you.

Ingredients

  • 250 gr of Mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tomatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Recipe instructions

  1. Cut the Mozzarella cheese in stripes and let them drain off, so you leave from them the water in excess.
  2. Wash the tomatoes and cut them in stripes like the Mozzarella cheese.
  3. Place the tomatoes and the Mozzarella cheese in an irregular way on the dish and season with oil and salt. Enjoy!
