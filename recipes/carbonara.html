Spaghetti Carbonara

The real Carbonara!

When you want to cook the best Carbonara, this is the recipe to go to.

Ingredients

  • 320 gr of Spaghetti
  • 6 eggs
  • 150 gr of 'Guanciale'
  • 150 gr of 'Pecorino romano' cheese
  • Pepper

Recipe instructions

  1. Put the water and the salt in a pot and then put it on the cooker.
  2. Cut the Guanciale in little cubes.
  3. Put the Guanciale in a little pot and cook it for 10 minutes. Don't burn it!
  4. Put the Spaghetti inside the water pot and let them cook for 9-10 minutes.
  5. Take the yellow part of the eggs in a little bowl, and mix them with the Pecorino cheese and the pepper.
  6. Take off the Spaghetti and add the Guanciale and the mixed eggs on them.
  7. Enjoy your Spaghetti 'Carbonara'!
