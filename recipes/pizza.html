Pizza Crust

The pizza made like in Naples!

When you want to cook the best pizza, this is the recipe to go to.

Ingredients for 3-4 pizzas

  • 300 gr of flour type '0'
  • 200 gr of Manitoba flour
  • 400 gr of water
  • 3 gr of fresh beer yeast
  • 1 teaspoon of extra virgin olive oil
  • 11 gr of salt
  • Half a teaspoon of sugar
  • 120 gr of tomatoes
  • 100 gr of mozzarella cheese

Recipe instructions

  1. Prepare the dough and divide it into little loaves.
  2. Start the oven.
  3. Prepare the seasoning by blending the tomatoes and cutting the mozzarella cheese in little cubes. Then mix them with salt and extra virgin olive oil.
  4. Put the seasoning on the pizza, after you have made the big crust.
  5. Put the pizza in the oven for 5 minutes. Then take it off and enjoy your pizza!
